Invoice Receipts — Step-by-Step Workflow

Receipt voucher (payment collection) flow from the dashboard customer quick action through invoice allocation, local persistence, and Zoho sync.

flowchart TD
    A([User taps a customer on Dashboard]) --> B[Client Operations Sheet opens]
    B --> C[Tap Receipt Voucher Payment action]
    C --> D[Open Receipt Payment Dialog]

    D --> E["Load customer open invoices
sort oldest first"]
    E --> F[Enter payment amount]
    F --> G["Auto-allocate amount across invoices
oldest first, capped at each balance"]
    G --> H[Select payment mode
Cash, Cheque, Bank Transfer, Card]
    H --> I{Adjust per-invoice
allocations manually?}
    I -- Yes --> J["Edit allocation amount per invoice
field error if above invoice balance"]
    J --> K
    I -- No --> K{"Form valid?
amount above zero AND
each alloc within balance AND
total allocated not over amount"}

    K -- No --> L[LOG RECEIPT disabled]
    L --> F

    K -- Yes --> M[Tap LOG RECEIPT]
    M --> N["Build ReceiptVoucher payload
temp id + temp payment number
allocations, amount, mode, reference"]
    N --> O[("Save receipt to local Hive
deduct customer outstanding instantly")]
    O --> P[("Enqueue SyncQueueItem
type receipt, status pending")]
    P --> Q[Trigger SyncWorker syncPendingItems]
    Q --> R["Close dialog
success snackbar
onPaymentLogged callback"]

    R --> S{Network available?}
    S -- No --> T[Item stays queued
retried on reconnect]
    S -- Yes --> U[POST payment to
Zoho Books via syncReceiptVoucher]
    U --> V{Zoho accepted?}
    V -- Yes --> W(["Mark synced
replace temp id with Zoho id"])
    V -- No --> X[Mark failed
stays in queue for retry]
